The trick is balancing canopy size, portability, and wind resistance, especially on a budget, where every dollar counts. When shopping on a budget, focus on key trade-offs: a smaller umbrella is easier to carry but shades less area; an integrated anchor adds stability but can add weight; tilt mechanisms improve sun-following but may reduce durability.
